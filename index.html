History

Background

The Orlando Magic are an American professional basketball team based in Orlando, Florida. The team was established in 1989.The Magic became the first ever major-league professional sports franchise in the Orlando area, following an expansion fee of reportedly $32.5 million. Since the creation of the team, the Magic have reached the NBA Finals twice (once in 1995 & in 2009).

Arena

The Orlando Magic play at the Amway Center, located in Downtown Orlando. The events center was named the Amway Center on August 5, 2008 and the building opened to the public for the first time on October 1, 2010.
